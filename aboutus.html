About ISU Fit. Empowering ISU students.

ISU Fit is a student-centric platform crafted to enhance the fitness journey of every Cyclone. Our mission is to provide effective workout plans, nutritious meal suggestions, and a comprehensive weight tracker to help students achieve their wellness goals while balancing academic life.
