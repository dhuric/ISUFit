Meal Ideas

Inspiration for every time of the day.

Breakfast Start your day right.

  • Oatmeal with fresh fruit and nuts
  • Whole grain toast with avocado and poached eggs
  • Smoothie with spinach, banana, protein powder, and almond milk

Lunch Keep it light and refreshing.

  • Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens and vinaigrette
  • Quinoa bowl with black beans, corn, avocado, and salsa
  • Turkey and hummus wrap with cucumber and sprouts

Dinner End the day with a satisfying meal.

  • Salmon fillets with roasted sweet potatoes and asparagus
  • Beef stir-fry with broccoli, bell peppers, and soy sauce
  • Vegetarian chili with beans, lentils, tomatoes, and spices
